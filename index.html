Visit Claremont

A charming college town at the foothills of the San Gabriel Mountains.

Top three activities to do in Claremont

Explore the Claremont Village

Walk through the heart of Claremont, filled with local cafés, bookstores, restaurants, and small shops.

Hike the Claremont Hills

Enjoy scenic trails with panoramic views of the valley. Perfect for hiking, running, or a peaceful sunset walk.

Visit the Claremont Colleges

Stroll through beautiful campuses known for architecture, art, and lively student culture.
